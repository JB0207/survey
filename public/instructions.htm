The following is an example of a simple survey implemented in nodeGame.

The survey is expected to take around - minutes to complete.

You will be asked for some basic demographics, financial information, and your opinion about socio-economic inequality in the US. Finally, you will answer some questions abour your political knowledge.

Please note that:

  • You must be at least 18 years old to participate in this survey.
  • You may press the Back button to navigate back in the survey, but your progress will be erased, and you will need to re-answer the questions as you step forward again.
  • You will be compensated for good faith participation in this experiment with $-USD. Submitted tasks will be rejected if they are completed in a manner that appears to be random, incomplete, or otherwise negligent.
If you have understood this introduction and agree with its content, press the "Next" button at the top of the screen to start the survey.
